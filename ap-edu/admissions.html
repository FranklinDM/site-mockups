A. Online Registration

  1. Visit and accomplish the registration form here.
  2. Upon successful registration, the Student Number, Branch number, Branch email address, Information on payments, and Requirements for submission will be displayed.
Important!
  • Email address of parent is required upon registration
  • Access the reminders and download the APEC Schools Admission forms.

B. Five Critical Requirements

  • Submit the following requirements to the email address provided upon successful registration
    • PSA Birth Certificate (or NSO)
    • Form 138 (Report card)
    • Proof of Admission Fee payment
    • Student Questionaire
    • Enrollment Policies Agreement
Important!
  • Original copies are required to be submitted during the Grand Enrollment schedule.
  • Access the reminders and download the APEC Schools Admission forms.

C. Payment Options

  1. Online
    • Dragon Pay – this payment option can be accessed in the enrollment confirmation page
  2. Payment Mobile Apps

  3. Payment Centers
